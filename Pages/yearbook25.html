RISD Yearbook 2024

for Rhode Island School of Design

Editorial / 2024


The 2025 yearbook is a celebration of what we carry with us and the moments we hold close. Each student page is personalized—decorated and curated like a proud, well-loved fridge door. We dedicate this yearbook as a keepsake for the graduating class of 2025. What’s yours?

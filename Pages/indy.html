The Indy

for The College Hill Independence

Editorial, Identity / 2023-2024


The College Hill Independent is a Providence-based publication written, illustrated, designed, and edited by students from Brown University and the Rhode Island School of Design.


As one of the design editors for Indy Vol 47 & 48, Ash is responsible for designer hiring, coordination between the creative and editor team, and the final organization of each. Collaboratively designed the identity and volume layout with Sam Stewart and Gina Kang.


They now work as SCHEMA Section Editor
